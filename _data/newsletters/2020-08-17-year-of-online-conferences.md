# Issue: #3 Year of online conferences

August 17th, 2020

### 🗞 Articles and Short Updates

-   [Berners-Lee 'sorry' for slashes](http://news.bbc.co.uk/2/hi/technology/8306631.stm) - Sir Tim Berners-Lee, the creator of the World Wide Web, has confessed that the // in a web address were actually "unnecessary"

-   [What does 100% mean in CSS?](https://wattenberger.com/blog/css-percents) - The basic of "100%" CSS value. Excellent summary with clear visual explainers.

-   [Polymorphism in JavaScript](https://zellwk.com/blog/polymorphism-javascript/) - explore what Polymorphism actually is in JavaScript

-   [Form design](https://gerireid.com/forms.html) - Best practice, research insights and examples for Form Design

-   [Reactivated](https://reactivated.app/) - open-source app that scans your JS dependencies and generates cool dashboards.

-   [Official Redux Essentials](https://redux.js.org/tutorials/essentials/part-1-overview-concepts) - Official Redux tutorial that will introduce you to Redux and teach you how to use it the right way, using our latest recommended tools and best practices.

-   [What’s New in Chrome DevTools in 2020](https://css-tricks.com/whats-new-in-devtools-2020/) - Brief introduction to features in Chrome DevTools in 2020.

-   [Promise.allSettled](https://davidwalsh.name/promise-allsettled) - by DavidWalsh, covering essentials of `Promises.allSettled`

-   [Jamstack Tools, APIs & Services Compared](https://www.sitepoint.com/jamstack-tools-services-apis/) - comprehensive list of services related to Jamstack development from deployment options to headless CMS, forms, identity solutions and more, with a useful comparison chart for each category.

-   [Why It's OK to Over-engineer Your Blog](https://www.freecodecamp.org/news/why-its-ok-to-overengineer-your-blog/) - An argument in favor of over-engineering your personal blog as a kind of laboratory where you can experiment with new technologies to learn.

-   [The amazing power of service workers](https://gomakethings.com/the-amazing-power-of-service-workers/) - Fun things that you could do with Service Workers on your site today
